Time (in seconds) to wait, after starting the VM, and (if selected) after the VMware tools have started.
  • If the slave is responsible for connecting to the master (e.g. JNLP) then this is the maximum amount of time that the master will wait for the slave to come online before timing out.
  • If the master is responsible for connecting to the slave (e.g. SSH) then this is the exact amount of time that the master will delay before commencing the connection process.
